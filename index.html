SLAB SPACE INDIA AND CO

We, Slab Space India and Co., are quarry owners, stone processors and exporters of quality granite blocks and slabs. The factory processes includes sawing the blocks into slabs, polishing, and providing textured finishes such as

  • Leather Flamed
  • Flamed and Brushed Honed

In the natural stone trade, quality is of prime importance. Our organisation is a vertically integrated one, providing us with the competitive advantage of closely monitoring the perfection of the product from start to finish. As we have our own quarries, we can ensure consistent supply of our colours in necessary finishes.

The products we manufacture are varied. We deal with

  • Slabs
  • Tiles
  • Cut-to-Size project orders

Slab Space India and Co began operations in the year 2016 with Mr Hariiharan as the Managing Partner under the guidance and supervision of his experienced father Mr S. Shankaranarayanan, a pioneer in the granite industry. Mr Shankaranarayanan began his business as a quarry owner/operator in the year 1988. He was one of the first to operate a Kashmir White quarry in Keelavaluvu, Madurai District, Tamil Nadu, India. Subsequently, he went on to operate various quarries in

Tamilnadu, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh (India) and Sri Lanka.In 2013 he took charge of a struggling granite processing unit in Hosur, Krishnagiri, Tamilnadu, India which has five gang saws, a resin line, a grinding machine, a polishing line, a calibration machine and a flaming machine.

Having high-quality products, processes and supervision at hand, we at Slab Space India and Co., are headed to tackle international markets, supplying to distributors and fabricators. Presently, we have exported to the USA, Canada, the UK, and a few other countries in Europe with plans to expand to several other countries shortly as per future requirements.
